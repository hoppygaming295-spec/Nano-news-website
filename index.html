Xmas decor ban only for halal-certified kitchens, restaurants, Melaka exco rep clarifies

Melaka state executive councillor Datuk Mohd Yadzil Yaakub has clarified that the recent ban on Christmas decorations only applies to halal-certified kitchens and restaurants.

He emphasized that non-halal establishments are not affected by this directive, which aims to maintain the sanctity of halal certification in food preparation areas.

"The ban is specifically for halal-certified kitchens and restaurants to ensure that the halal standards are upheld during the festive season," he said in a statement.

Mohd Yadzil also urged the public to respect the guidelines set forth by the authorities and to celebrate the festive season in a manner that is considerate of all communities.
